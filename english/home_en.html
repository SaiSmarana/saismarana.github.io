When one fervently offers prayer with utmost earnestness
And enters the exalted realm of thy divine proximity,
Thou dost shower thine abundant grace ceaselessly Oh! revered Sadguru Sai!
May each moment I breathe be sanctified by thy hallowed service!
May every thought of mine be filled with thy divine essence!
May every word I utter be imbued with thy holy tales!
May every sound that reaches my ears resonate with thy divine name!
May every movement of my hands be a humble salute to thy magnificence!
May every step I take serve as a circumambulation to your sacred presence!
May the imprints of thy holy feet be etched on the canvas of my heart!
May thy divine essence permeate me entirely obliterating my very being!
Oh, venerable Baba! This is my humble prayer to you!
    Pen: Sri Vijay Kishore
